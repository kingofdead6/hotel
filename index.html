Hotel Monte Cristo

A Parisian story

Alexandre DUMAS is, like Victor Hugo, one of the most important 19th century French writers. In addition to being
a novelist he was a playwright, politician, adventurer, seducer, cook, traveller ...

He embraced experience and yearned to try everything!

Our hotel is an updated representation of what the great man's home might have been like had he been working
today.
